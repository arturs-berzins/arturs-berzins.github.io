About me

I am a research fellow at the Institute for Machine Learning at JKU Linz with Sepp Hochreiter and Johannes Brandstetter. Currently, I am finishing my PhD in Mathematics awarded by the University of Oslo. I spent the first three years of my PhD at the Geometry group at SINTEF as a fellow of GRAPES — a Marie Sklodowska-Curie ITN on Learning, processing and optimising shapes.

I received my undergrad in mechanical engineering from RWTH Aachen specializing in simulation technology. In both of my theses, I applied ML to engineering tasks where geometry was a key consideration. This inspired my current research which includes

  • geometry in machine learning for theory and application,
  • machine learning for science and engineering,
  • ReLU neural networks,
  • neural fields.
