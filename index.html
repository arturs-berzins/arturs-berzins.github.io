About me

I am currently at the Geometry group of SINTEF pursuing a PhD in Mathematics from the University of Oslo.
I am a fellow of GRAPES, a Marie Sklodowska-Curie Innovative Training Network.

I received my BSc and MSc in mechanical engineering from RWTH Aachen specializing in simulation technology.
In my theses, I applied machine learning to engineering workflows where geometry was key.
This let to my current work on geometry in ML for both theory and application.
