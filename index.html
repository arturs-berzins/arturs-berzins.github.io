About me

I am currently at the Geometry group at SINTEF pursuing a PhD in Mathematics from the University of Oslo. I am a fellow of GRAPES, a Marie Sklodowska-Curie Innovative Training Network.

I received my BSc and MSc in mechanical engineering from RWTH Aachen specializing in simulation technology. In both of my theses, I applied ML to engineering tasks where geometry was a key consideration. This inspired my current research on geometry in ML for both theory and application.
